About Me

My name is Spencer Vaughan, nice to meet you! I am an Atlanta-native excited to start a new career in the world of programming. I graduated from Georgia State University in 2018 with a B.B.A. from the J. Mack Robinson College of Business, after spending 3 years as a D.J. and manager of the acclaimed radio station, WRAS - Album 88. While music was always a passion, it was rarely a paycheck. I’ve since been bartending at the historic Manuel’s Tavern for the last 2 years and when the restaurants shut down due to COVID-19, it felt like time to pursue new skills and an exciting career.


You could say I’m a relative beginner to coding, which would be true, however I feel that title fails to recognize my 3 years of experience editing html on Myspace when I was in middle school.
